
\subsection{Rank of Auto Threshold Methods in ImageJ (Based on inlens, contrast, nice image)}

We tested all auto threshold methods provided by ImageJ on different SEM images of nanoparticles, including nice, uneven contrast and inlens images. The results suggest that \best{Local Otsu} is the \best{best} choise. Meanwhile, \good{Local Bersen} and \good{Local MidGrey} are alternative options, which are working \good{good}.\\

It is also important to avoid using \worse{Local Median}, \worse{Local Phansalker}, \worse{Local Sauvola}, \worse{Global MinError(I)} and \worse{Global Percentitle}. They can not give resonable threshold when analyze SEM images of nanopartilces. \\

By analyzing the thresholded images carefully,  we summize the effectiveness of these auto methods in the following tables, including 9 local methods and 16 global ones.\\
